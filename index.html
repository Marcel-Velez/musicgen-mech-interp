Interchange interventions

For each of the following prompts, we randomly chose one of the 100 original prompts we intervened on. We provide the prompt text, original audio, the desired concept, and the best- and worst-scoring interventions for this specific prompt. The best-scoring intervention is the one that most closely matches the desired concept or removes the original concept the most, while the worst-scoring intervention is the one that most closely matches the original concept or the original concept even increased after intervening. There is no ordering based on intervention effectiveness between different desired concepts for a given original concept
